My First Blog Post

Hello World

This is my first blog post in what I hope will be a successful journey.
I’d been putting this off for about a year now, ever since I first thought of the idea.

My constant excuse was that I will begin when I have acquired enough knowledge.
But few minutes ago, I was reading a blog post written by someone on a 100 day coding challenge and was writing down his experiences.
The post was only a few lines long, and the realization hit me: I will never acquire enough knowledge.
This software development field I’ve devoted myself to is a field of constant, incessant and never ending learning.
I’ve read severally about giving out as much (or more) than you take in, but I’ve just begun to understand what this means.

This post isn’t going to be long, I just want to get something down before I discourage myself again. It is my hope to write something down
each day about what I learnt, and my life experiences. I know that as I keep writing down my experiences, my writing style will also improve.

So hold on, sit tight. It’s going to be a long long journey…

Simeon.
